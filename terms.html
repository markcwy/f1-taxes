Complicated Terms

TAXES

Taxes are a compulsory contribution to state revenue, taken by the government on workers' income and business profits, or added to the cost of some goods, services, and transactions. There are a few kinds of taxes.

However, as an F-1 Visa holder, you are not required to pay some of these taxes.

TAX WITHHOLDING

This is the amount of money held from your income by your employer to pay for taxes.

TAX RETURNS

Sometimes, your employer deducts more than the amount you owe. You can claim back this amount from the IRS. These are your tax returns.
