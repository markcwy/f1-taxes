Tax Information for F-1 Visas

A GUIDE FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

As an F-1 Visa holder, you have certain tax obligations to fulfill during your time in the United States.

This is a simplified guide, but be warned that you are using it at your own risk, and it is recommended that you contact a qualified accountant to help and guide you through your questions.

What forms are there?

8843

Filing this form is mandatory.

It lets the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) know if you are a non-resident alien or a resident alien for tax purposes, and is especially important if you plan to work and live in the U.S. in the future.

WHAT KIND OF ALIEN AM I?

1080NR/NR-EZ

Filing this form is mandatory if you have earned income during the year.

This form is for filing federal tax returns.
